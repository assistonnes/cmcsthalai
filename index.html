PATHIANIN MIN HRIATCHIANZIA

– Lt. Col. Lalliankunga

Pathian hian a thilsiam leh a enkawlna hnuaia awmte hi min ve en ngunin min va hre chiang dawn teh lul em!

Matthew 10:29-33 Chawngzawng pahnih dar tangka pakhatah an hralh thin lo vem ni Nimahsela, an zinga pakhat mah in Pa lovin leiah a tla lo vang.

Mihring chauh ni lovin sava te tak te pawh a ngaihsak a, an chetzia tin reng pawh a hriatpui a ni.

In samzaite lam pawh hi chhiar vek a ni asin Chuvangin, hlau suh u; chawngzawng tam tak aiin in hlu zawk a ni.

Nitina i chenpui, i hmangaih leh ngainat ber; mi pakhat chauh pawh ni se, a samzai neih zat i hriat sak em?

Chuvangin he thu hi vawng reng ang u: "Chutichuan, mite hmaa mi tan apiang, ka Pa vana mi hmaah ka tan ve ang."
